About Krishna Speech & Hearing Clinic

Dedicated to Your Hearing Health

Krishna Speech and Hearing Clinic has been serving the communities of Hubballi and Dharwad with professional hearing care and speech therapy services. Our experienced audiologists and speech therapists are committed to improving your quality of life through better hearing and communication.

We understand that hearing loss affects not just the individual but the entire family. That's why we provide comprehensive care that includes detailed assessments, personalized treatment plans, and ongoing support for both patients and their families.

Our state-of-the-art equipment and modern facilities like, PURE TONE AUDIOMETRY, BERA, OAE, IMPEDANCE and much more ensure accurate hearing loss and effective treatment for all types of hearing and speech disorders.

Premium Hearing Aid Solutions

Behind-the-Ear (BTE) Hearing Aids

Powerful and versatile hearing aids suitable for all degrees of hearing loss. Comfortable design with advanced digital processing technology.

  • Suitable for all hearing loss levels
  • Easy to handle and maintain
  • Long battery life
  • Multiple program options

In-the-Ear (ITE) Hearing Aids

Custom-made hearing aids that fit comfortably in your ear. Discreet appearance with excellent sound quality and user-friendly controls.

  • Custom-fitted for comfort
  • Discreet appearance
  • Easy-to-use controls
  • Natural sound quality

Digital Hearing Aids

Advanced digital technology with noise reduction, feedback cancellation, and automatic environment adaptation for superior hearing experience.

  • Digital signal processing
  • Noise reduction technology
  • Automatic volume adjustment
  • Multiple listening programs

Rechargeable Hearing Aids

Convenient rechargeable hearing aids with long-lasting battery life. No need for battery replacements with overnight charging capability.

  • Rechargeable technology
  • All-day battery life
  • Environmentally friendly
  • Easy charging solutions

Invisible-In the-Canal (IIC) Hearing Aids

Custom-made hearing aids that fit comfortably in your ear for students and people with active life styles as it stays secure.

  • Tiny in size
  • Unlimited re-programmable
  • Best in Performance
  • Reduces "Occlusion effect" (the blocked-ear feeling)

Completely-in-Canal (CIC) Hearing Aids

A CIC Hearing Aid is tiny device that fits deep in the ear canal, offering discreet and natural hearing

  • Almost invisible
  • Natural sound
  • Custom fit
  • Wind noise reduction
