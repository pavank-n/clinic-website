About Krishna Speech & Hearing Clinic

Dedicated to Your Hearing Health

Krishna Speech and Hearing Clinic has been serving the communities of Hubballi and Dharwad with professional hearing care and speech therapy services. Our experienced audiologists and speech therapists are committed to improving your quality of life through better hearing and communication.
